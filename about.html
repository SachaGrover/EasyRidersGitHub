About us

Welcome to the Easy Riders Skate Shop, the online shop for custom Concrete Snowboard parts to make your ride even closer to snowboarding. We offer highly durable castors which support a wider center wheel. The primary advantages to these center wheels are the improved sensations when riding on uneven terrain and improved stability when center-wheeling.

For the time being the "Easy Riders" castor we offer is targetted solely for downhill, not for freestyle. Our products come with neoprene sheets to further improve the lifespan the products and ensure an even smoother ride than standard castors.
